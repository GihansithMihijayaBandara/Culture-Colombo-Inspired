About Culture Colombo

Tradition, Rituals & Modern Taste

Our Story

Culture Colombo was born with the intent to bring tradition, rituals, and modern taste to Sri Lankan cuisine. Designed to be a culinary epicenter, we uphold the warmth of the local household while celebrating the vibrant flavors of Sri Lanka.

Our Philosophy

We take pride in sourcing fresh, seasonal ingredients—supporting local farms and communities. Whether it’s fiery hot curries or soothing buffalo curd, every dish is crafted with care and a twist of creativity.

Our Space

Step into a colonial-inspired Walawwa setting, where vintage charm meets modern comfort. Our mural walls and interior design reflect a cultural infusion that invites you to feel at home while you dine.

Our Promise

We’re here to offer more than just a meal—we offer an experience. One that’s authentically Sri Lankan, thoughtfully prepared, and warmly served.
